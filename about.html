Our Technology

Built on cutting-edge AI and web technologies

Real-ESRGAN for Image Enhancement

We use state-of-the-art Real-ESRGAN models converted to TensorFlow Lite for browser-based inference, delivering professional image upscaling and enhancement without server uploads.

RMBG 2.0 for Background Removal

Advanced AI segmentation models provide pixel-perfect background removal with accurate edge detection, even for complex subjects like hair and fur.

WebGPU & WASM Acceleration

We leverage WebGPU for GPU-accelerated inference and WebAssembly for high-performance computation, ensuring fast processing times on modern browsers.

Client-Side Processing

All AI models run entirely in your browser using TensorFlow.js. Your files are processed locally and never uploaded to our servers or any third party.
