How do I report a bug?

Please use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as the subject. Include as much detail as possible about the issue, including your browser version and steps to reproduce.

Can I request a new feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Submit your feature request using the form with the subject "Feature Request" and we'll review it for future updates.

Do you offer enterprise solutions?

Yes! For enterprise licensing, custom integrations, or white-label solutions, please contact us at business@aitools.com.

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Technical support requests are prioritized and typically answered within 24 hours.
